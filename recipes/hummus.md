# Hummus

## Ingredients

- 1 tin chickpeas, drained
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1 lemon quarter, squeezed
- 1/2 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp pepper
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 3 tbsp sesame oil
- 2 tbsp warm water

## Preparation

Add all of the ingredients to the food processor and blend into a smooth paste.
